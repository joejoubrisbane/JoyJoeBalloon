Frequently Asked Questions

How much does balloon twisting cost in Brisbane?

Joy Joe's 2-hour balloon twisting party package starts from $320. A travel fee applies based on your distance from Sunnybank.

How many kids can Joy Joe entertain in 2 hours?

Up to 20 children in a 2-hour session with custom designs. Larger groups can be accommodated — contact Joe for a quote.

What areas does Joy Joe cover?

Joy Joe services a 35km radius from Sunnybank, covering most of Brisbane including the northside, southside, and western suburbs.

How do I book Joy Joe for my child's birthday?

Simply send a message via the contact page or Facebook with your date, suburb, approximate number of kids, and start time.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as possible for weekends, especially school holidays and the Christmas/end-of-year season. Last-minute bookings are sometimes available — just ask!

What balloon designs can Joy Joe make?

As an experienced Brisbane balloon twister, Joy Joe can create 48 popular designs including Hello Kitty, Spider-Man, Pikachu, Paw Patrol, unicorns, dinosaurs, and more. Custom requests are welcome!

Does Joy Joe do corporate events?

Yes! Balloon twisting entertainment is perfect for corporate events, birthday parties, school fetes, and community celebrations across Brisbane.
